📐 Methodology — how iTV is calculated (Full Month view)

iTV formula (all journeys): iTV = (Test_avg_TV_per_user − Control_avg_TV_per_user) × Test_user_count

This dashboard — Full Calendar Month:

  • Window: entire calendar month. For MP, gated to start at PROVISION_CAMPAIGN_START (Apr 9) since campaign hadn't launched before that.
  • Aggregation: Test_total_TV / Test_user_count − Control_total_TV / Control_user_count → multiplied by Test_user_count.

MP cohorts (two views):

  • MP OneSignal: push-reachable subset — 217K iOS Test + 24K Control + 333K Android Test + 37K Control. From OneSignal segments.
  • MP Full (BQ): full data-team cohort — 755,624 Target + 84,075 Control. From bigquery-369506.SG_YOUTRIP_PUBLIC.TEMP_MOBILE_SPEND_20260406. Reproduces data team's $264,468 SGD iTV within $34 SGD rounding.
