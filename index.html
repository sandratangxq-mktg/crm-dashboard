📐 Methodology — how iTV is calculated (Cohort × Window view)

iTV formula (all journeys): iTV = (Test_avg_TV_per_user − Control_avg_TV_per_user) × Test_user_count

This dashboard — Cohort × Measurement-Window:

  • Cohort: daily new entrants. Each day a user enters a journey segment, they form a cohort with the entry date.
  • Window: measure their TV in the next X days post-entry only — LB = 3 days, GEO = 7 days, MP = 22 days (Apr 9 → Apr 30, single full-campaign window).
  • Aggregation: for each (segment, month) sum all entry-date cohorts → aggregate user_count and TV → compute Test_avg − Control_avg → multiply by total Test count. Matches dashboard's historical method.
  • Use case: attributes lift to specific journey events. More conservative — excludes downstream TV beyond the measurement window.

Filters by journey:

  • GEO, LB: TXN_TYPE = 'Fin' (settled card spend, single-counted; excludes Auth/AuthRev paired-twins, P2P, Remittance, Bank Withdrawals, system ops).
  • MP OneSignal, MP Full (BQ): wallet-only filter — DIGITAL_WALLET IS NOT NULL AND msg_type IN ('AUTHADV','FINADV') AND txn_category IN ('POS','eCommerce') AND tlog_id NOT IN VIEW_REVERSE_TXN. Matches data team's #503 dashboard exactly.

MP cohorts (two views):

  • MP OneSignal: push-reachable subset — 217K iOS Test + 24K Control + 333K Android Test + 37K Control. From OneSignal segments.
  • MP Full (BQ): full data-team cohort — 755,624 Target + 84,075 Control. From bigquery-369506.SG_YOUTRIP_PUBLIC.TEMP_MOBILE_SPEND_20260406. Reproduces data team's $264,468 SGD iTV within $34 SGD rounding.

* = estimated value (journey was revamped in March 26).
